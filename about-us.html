About Us

Globe beaches is a movement that aims to bring people together into a dynamic and vibrant community of beach lovers. Our goal is to motivate people to share and exchange their individual beach life experiences and information to help us create a global ‘library’ of memories that would guide other beach life seekers on how to design and enjoy the ultimate next beach escape.

By sharing your memories with Globe Beaches, you inspire and motivate other community members to make their own unforgettable memories by the beach.

We want to spread love and positive vibes with everyone around the globe.
